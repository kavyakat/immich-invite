Something not working? 🛠️

The page won't load

Open the Tailscale app and confirm it's connected (green icon). If it looks off, sign out and back in, or toggle the switch.

"Site can't be reached" error

Double-check the URL is http://100.79.182.10:2283 — that's http, not https. If it's right and still failing, ping Kavya — his PC might be off.

Sign-up disabled or "awaiting approval"

Just message Kavya — he can enable registration or approve you from the admin panel in seconds.

I never got the Tailscale invite email

Check spam. Then check spam again. Then poke Kavya.
